\section{SOLUTION}
Given points are (-4,3), and slope $m=\frac{1}{2}$

The line formula in matrix form
\begin{align}
n^\top x&=c\\
\end{align}
\begin{align}
n&=\myvec{m\\-1}\\
&=\myvec{\frac{1}{2}\\-1}\\
c&=\frac{mx_1-y_1}{2}\\
&=2 \myvec{\frac{1}{2}\times-4-3}\\
&=\frac{-10}{2}\\
&=-5
\end{align}

\begin{align}
n^\top x&=c\\
\myvec{\frac{1}{2}\\-1}^\top x&=-5\\
&=x-2y=-10\\
\implies x-2y+10=0
\end{align}
